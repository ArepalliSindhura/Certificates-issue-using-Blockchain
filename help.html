About metamask and ganache

  • Metamask is the E-wallet that we use to perform ethereum transactions.
  • We use ganache in this case to get default addresses and ethers.
  • Every ganache account is given 100 ethers which is a default.

Instructions to use this application

  • Step 1 : enter into the local host.
  • Step 2 : click on Certificate issue to view the certificates and block a certificate.
  • Step 3 : Now, you have to login into your metamask account by clicking on the link provided in this page.
  • Step 4 : Before logging into your metamask account you must add the metamask extension to your chrome. This will just take a few seconds.
  • Step 5 : Once you login into your account with your secret code and password you will be able to block a certificate.
  • Step 6 : Come back to the website and clicl on the issue button below your memo.
  • Step 7 : Your memo is blocked with your id.
  • Step 8 : You can also view the details of students by clicking on students list.
